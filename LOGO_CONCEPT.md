## Logo Concepts

### Primary Logo Concept: "The Busy Bee"

**Main Visual:**
A friendly, modern cartoon bee character with clean lines and personality.

**Key Elements:**
- **Bee Body**: Round, chunky body with classic yellow and black stripes (3 stripes)
- **Wings**: Translucent, slightly iridescent wings with subtle gradient
- **Face**: Large friendly eyes (slightly oversized for cuteness), gentle smile
- **Accessories**: Small round glasses (optional, adds "smart" vibe)
- **Holding**: Tiny PDF document or folder in one hand/leg
- **Antennae**: Curved, with small balls at the tips

**Style:**
- Modern flat design with subtle shadows
- Rounded, friendly shapes (no sharp edges)
- Clean vector illustration
- Works in both color and monochrome

**Color Palette:**

**Primary Colors:**
- **Bee Yellow**: `#FDB913` (warm, energetic gold)
- **Bee Black**: `#1A1A1A` (deep charcoal, not pure black)
- **Honey Gold**: `#F5A623` (accent for highlights)

**Secondary Colors:**
- **Sky Blue**: `#4A90E2` (trust, professionalism)
- **Paper White**: `#FAFAFA` (clean backgrounds)
- **Success Green**: `#7ED321` (completed tasks)

**Accent Colors:**
- **Honeycomb Orange**: `#FF9500` (warm highlights)
- **Pollen Yellow**: `#FFD700` (bright accents)

## Typography

**Primary Font: "Poppins" or "Nunito"**
- Modern, friendly, rounded sans-serif
- Excellent readability
- Professional yet approachable
- Free and widely available

**Wordmark Style:**
- **"Lease"** in regular weight
- **"Bee"** in bold weight
- Optional: Slight yellow/gold color on "Bee" portion
- Letter spacing: Slightly loose for friendliness

**Alternative Font: "Quicksand" or "Comfortaa"**
- Even more playful/rounded
- Use if going for maximum friendliness

**Body Font: "Inter" or "Open Sans"**
- For UI, documentation, and marketing copy
- Clean, professional, highly legible

## Bee Character Design Details

### Expression & Personality
- **Happy but focused** - Not overly silly
- **Hardworking** - Determined expression
- **Friendly** - Approachable, welcoming
- **Smart** - Optional glasses add intelligence vibe

### Poses for Different Uses

**Standard Pose (Primary Logo):**
- Front-facing, slightly angled
- One wing visible
- Holding a document/folder
- Gentle smile

**Flying Pose (Loading/Processing):**
- Angled diagonally upward
- Both wings visible, slightly blurred for motion
- Trail of sparkles or dots behind
- Use for: Loading animations, "processing" states

**Celebrating Pose (Success States):**
- Arms/legs raised in celebration
- Big smile
- Confetti or stars around
- Use for: Completion screens, success messages

**Thinking Pose (AI Processing):**
- Hand/leg on chin
- Thought bubble with document icon
- Slightly puzzled but determined
- Use for: "Analyzing..." screens

## Logo Usage Guidelines

### Do's âœ…
- Use on white or very light backgrounds primarily
- Maintain clear space around logo (at least 50% of bee's height)
- Use official color palette
- Scale proportionally
- Use PNG with transparency for digital
- Use SVG when possible for scalability

### Don'ts âŒ
- Don't stretch or distort the logo
- Don't change the colors (except approved variations)
- Don't add effects (drop shadows, glows, etc.)
- Don't place on busy backgrounds without white container
- Don't rotate the logo
- Don't separate the bee from "LeaseBee" text in primary logo

### Minimum Sizes
- **Full Logo**: 100px width minimum
- **Icon Only**: 24px minimum (32px recommended)
- **Favicon**: 16x16px, 32x32px, 64x64px versions
